What is Songpinion?

 

Can you relate at all? You find a new song and you really like it for some reason: Wether it's because of the lyrics, the instrumental or simply because of the vibe. You give your best, not to forget the aspects of why you like a song so much, but of course you end up forgetting it.

So, what can you do? Write it down your thoughts! If you collect all your songs, you don't just create your own catalogue, you'll also be able to analyse, what exactly you like about music. What are you waiting for? Tell us about your music and your taste.

Give your opinion with:


Star ratings


Remarks


Comments

FAQ

In order to submit a song, you need to fill out each and every field, otherwise your opinion will not be seen as valid thus it won't be saved in our database. After submitting, your data will be lost if you didn't fill every field. We're currently working on a resolution.

You've encountered another bug! If you want to edit something, you have to make sure, that every field is filled out. Unfortunately, while editing your opinion, the field "Release Date" will become empty, even if you had filled it with a date before. So please enter while editing the release date again.

It's very simple:

  • First, you need to know some facts about your song: its genre, title, artist, the album (if it's a single, simply enter the song title with "Single" in the end) and of course the release date.
  • Now you can talk about your opinion about the song: What do you like? How many stars would you give it? What do you dislike? Feel free to enter every single thought that comes to your mind when thinking about the song.
